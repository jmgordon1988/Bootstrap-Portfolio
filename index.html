About Me

My name is John and I have a bachelor’s degree in Film and Media Studies (UCI, graduation 2010). In recent years, I have decided that I would like to expand my skillset and pursue a career in web development to provide both stability as well as expand my creative output, and as such I attended a coding bootcamp at UCI November 2018-February 2019.

I have a passion for problem solving and critical thinking. I also really enjoy storytelling and creativity, especially thinking outside the box to come up with new solutions. Recently won an award for ‘Most Awe-Inspiring’ and ‘Most Creative’ for project work on a ‘Mad Libs’ style game.

I work well with others and am an excellent communicator, thriving in group environments. Quick learner, always seeking to learn new technologies and programs.

Proficient in HTML, CSS, Javascript, JQuery, MongoDB, React, and Ajax. Whatever the technology may be, I always strive to do my best and constantly improve, and will be a strong asset in any team environment.
